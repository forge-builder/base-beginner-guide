Base Starter Guide

Your first steps into the onchain world

1
Get a Wallet

You'll need a crypto wallet to interact with Base. A wallet is like your digital identity on the blockchain ‚Äî it holds your funds and lets you sign transactions.

Recommended: Coinbase Wallet or MetaMask. Both work great with Base.

Get Coinbase Wallet Get MetaMask
2
Add Base Network

Base is a Layer 2 network built on Ethereum. You need to add it to your wallet to use dApps on Base.

  • Open your wallet settings
  • Look for "Networks" or "Add Network"
  • Search for "Base" or use the automatic setup below
Base Network Setup Guide
3
Get Some ETH

You need ETH (Ethereum's native token) to pay for transactions on Base. The good news: Base transactions cost less than a cent!

How to get ETH:

  • Buy from Coinbase and withdraw to your Base address
  • Use a bridge (like Across or Stargate) to move ETH from Ethereum mainnet
  • Ask a friend to send you some!
Use Base Bridge
4
Make Your First Transaction

Now the fun begins! Try a small transaction to understand how it works.

Try these on Base (testnet first!):

  • Send a small amount of ETH to another wallet
  • Swap tokens on a DEX like Uniswap
  • mint an NFT on Base

Security Tips

NEVER share your seed phrase with anyone! Anyone who asks for it is trying to scam you.

  • Your seed phrase = your money. Keep it offline, safe.
  • Always verify the website URL before connecting your wallet
  • Start with small amounts until you understand how it works
  • When in doubt, ask in the community before signing
